      Authors

      Several persons contributed to this website. These are in alphabetically order:

      Ahmed Alia

      Civil Safety Research (IAS-7) - Forschungszentrum Jülich

      Researcher at Forschungszentrum Jülich, specializing in artificial intelligence for complex data analytics and modeling.

             

      Thomas Chatagnon

      Civil Safety Research (IAS-7) - Forschungszentrum Jülich

      Researcher in crowd dynamics and pedestrian movement, specializing in balance recovery mechanisms in dense crowds and developing innovative data collection methodologies through his work with JupPedSim and PedPy.

             

      Mohcine Chraibi

      Civil Safety Research (IAS-7) - Forschungszentrum Jülich

      Head of the division Pedestrian Dynamics - Modeling at the IAS-7. Research intrests include Modeling, Simulation, Wayfinding, Software Engineering, Validation, Verification

             

      Alessandro Corbetta

      Department of Applied Physics and Science Education at Eindhoven University of Technology

      Assistant Professor at the Department of Applied Physics and Science Education at Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands, leading the group AI for traffic and complex flows

             

      Claudio Feliciani

      Research Center for Advanced Science and Technology (RCAST), University of Tokyo

      Project Associate Professor at Mathematical Physics of Emergent Systems. Interested in explaining the dynamics of complex systems by covering a wide range of fields and examining the problems under different perspectives.

           

      Milad Haghani

      University of New South Wales (UNSW)

      Senior Lecturer, UNSW Sydney. Expert in transport safety emphasizing human factors, with areas of interest including crowd safety, evacuation planning, transport psychology, econometric modelling, and meta-research.

           

      Kai Kratz

      Independent open source contributor

           

      Mira Küpper

      Architecture and Civil Engineering, University of Wuppertal

      Post-doctoral Researcher. Specializes in waiting pedestrians at train stations.

         

      Enrico Ronchi

      Department of Building & Environmental Technology, LUND Unversity, Sweden

      Associate Professor in Lund University. Specialist in Evacuation Modelling, Fire Safety Engineering, Human Behaviour in Fire, Pedestrian Modelling, Virtual Reality, WUI Emergencies, Road Safety and Tunnel Safety.

           

      Tobias Schrödter

      Independent open source contributor

      Innovation Engineer Software/Hardware

           

      Jette Degenhardt

      Civil Safety Research (IAS-7) - Forschungszentrum Jülich

      Post-doctoral Researcher. Specializes in application-oriented pedestrian simulations, the interplay between pedestrian streams and urban traffic, crowd movement analysis, and research software development.

           

      Antoine Tordeux

      Traffic Safety and Reliability, University of Wuppertal

      Jun.- Prof. Dr. Antoine Tordeux on the complex system of road traffic. Specializes in Applied Mathematics, Traffic Flow, Self-driven particle systems, and Pedestrian Dynamics.

         

      Ezel Üsten

      Civil Safety Research (IAS-7) - Forschungszentrum Jülich

      PhD researcher in the division of Pedestrian Dynamics - Social Psychology at IAS-7, specializing in motivation, emotion, and behavior in crowd entrance scenarios.

           

      Jun Zhang

      State Key Laboratory of Fire Science of University of Science and Technology of China

      Professor at USTC with focus on pedestrian and evacuation dynamics, encompassing pedestrian modeling, movement analysis techniques, and the study of dynamics in heterogeneous pedestrian flows, with particular attention to the elderly and disabled.
